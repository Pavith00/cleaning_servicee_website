Brightcleen (Pvt) Ltd, is one of the medium scale Janitorial Services company in Sri Lanka. It was established in 2005 with 05 clients and around 08 employees. Later on company grows little by little and earned 108 clients in 2010-2011 period. In 2015 Brightcleen earned 300 clients. Now it has over 400 clients across Sri Lanka and employs more than 500 employees. Brightcleen opened its 1st sub office in Galle. We develop our employees Knowledge, Skills and Attitudes through training programs that helps to provide the best services for our valuable Clients and Customers
